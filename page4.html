Code of Ethics

Sterling Investment Advisors, LLC believes:
·         Good stewardship and high ethical standards are necessary for client trust and confidence
to be secured and the public interest to be served. 
·         High ethical principles and professional standards are essential to positive outcomes. Rules
and regulations, while necessary, are not sufficient by themselves.
 
To this end, Sterling Investment Advisors, LLC has adopted the:
·         Asset Manager Code of Professional Conduct – for the entire firm
·         Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct – for individuals
