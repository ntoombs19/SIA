Code of Ethics

Sterling Investment Advisors, LLC believes:
·         Good stewardship and high ethical standards are necessary for client trust and confidence to be secured and the public interest to be served.
·         High ethical principles and professional standards are essential to positive outcomes.
·         Rules and regulations, while necessary, are not sufficient by themselves.
 
To this end, Sterling Investment Advisors, LLC has adopted the:
·         CFA Institute Asset Manager Code of Professional Conduct – for the entire firm
·         CFA Institute Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct – for individuals
